What camera perspective?

This shapes the whole visual feel of the world

A — Isometric

Fixed 45° angle, no perspective distortion. Like the screenshot you shared. Clean, readable at all scales.

B — 3D Perspective

Free camera with tilt and zoom. More immersive street-level views. Camera tilts flatter as you zoom out.

C — Top-Down 2D

Pure overhead view. Simpler to render. Great for zoomed-out city/country/world views. Less impressive at street level.
