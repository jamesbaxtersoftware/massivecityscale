How do zoom levels transition?

As you scroll out from street → block → city → planet, how does the world change?

Each zoom threshold snaps to a completely different visual representation

A — Discrete LOD Levels

Snap between distinct representations at thresholds. Street = detailed buildings. City = colored tiles. Country = outlines. Planet = a sphere. Each level is its own scene.

Same geometry scales continuously — buildings shrink until they become pixels/colors

B — Continuous Smooth Zoom

Everything exists at once and just gets smaller. Buildings become dots, dots become city blobs, blobs become a continent shape. No discrete snapping.

Smooth zoom within levels, cross-fade when switching representations

C — Hybrid (Recommended)

Distinct representations per scale level, but smooth cross-fade between them. Best of both — each level looks intentional, transitions feel fluid.
