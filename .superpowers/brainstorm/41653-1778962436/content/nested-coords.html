Nested Coordinate Spaces

Each scale level is a world within a world — like Russian dolls

The key insight

A road at position (4, 2) inside its street space doesn't need to know it's orbiting a sun 200 units away.

Each layer only stores small numbers relative to its parent. The GPU never sees numbers like 200,000,004.0 — which would cause shimmering.

What you see as you zoom
Street space active → buildings, roads, kerbs
City space active → city blocks as coloured boxes
Planet space active → city as a dot on a sphere
Solar space active → planet orbiting a sun
In Bevy terms

Each space is just a parent entity with children. Bevy's transform system handles composing them. You zoom by switching which parent's coordinate system the camera "lives in."
